Hover

A Travel Agent In Your Pocket

Hover is a travel agent in your pocket. Hover provides a platform that allows the user to plan a trip from A-Z. Starting with where they wish to go, how they will get there, where they will stay once they arrive, and even what they will do while they are there.

The Problem

The idea was to try and understand what exactly causes people to not follow through with their travel plans and why they don't like the planning process. By asking users what they find difficult, stressful, or simply unenjoyable, we hoped we could narrow down why the process is discouraging.

From the interviews, I concluded that context switching was a major contributror to why poeple fell through on there travel plans. Having to bounce from website to website, app to app, individuals would become overwhelmed with the amount of options available to them.

By providing a platform that keeps the user engaged in a sinlge application, we can eliminate context switcing, thus more people planning trips, going and seeing new palces, and spending money in places they other wise wouldnt, boosting economies around the world.
